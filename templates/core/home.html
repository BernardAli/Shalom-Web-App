About Shalom Baptist Church

Shalom Baptist Church sprang from Ash-Town Baptist church. In May 1994, the church was started at the Wesco Practice School.

Our History

Mision

Our purpose is to exalt God and to honour our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ, carry out his command to make disciples of all nations and also teach them to obey all that He commands (Matt 28:18-20) and to grow into maturity.

Vision

Be people-friendly, creative and have inspiring worship through teaching and preaching that is biblical and relevant to life. Have vital and supportive fellowship through the creation of outreach opportunities into the communities, district, municipality, metropolis, and nation in service and evangelism. Also have an auditorium designed to minister to the total person-spiritually, emotionally, physically and socially set in a peaceful inspiring garden.
